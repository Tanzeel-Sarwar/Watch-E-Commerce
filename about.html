About Benetti

Crafting Timeless Elegance Since 1924

Our Story

Benetti was founded in 1924 with a vision to create timepieces that blend traditional craftsmanship with innovative design. For nearly a century, we have been dedicated to the art of watchmaking, producing exquisite timepieces that stand the test of time.

Our commitment to quality and precision has made Benetti a respected name in the world of luxury watches. Each Benetti watch is a testament to our heritage and a symbol of enduring style.

Our Values

Craftsmanship

We take pride in the meticulous attention to detail that goes into every Benetti timepiece.

Innovation

While respecting tradition, we constantly push the boundaries of watchmaking technology.

Elegance

Our designs embody timeless sophistication, suitable for any occasion.
